The Maradona

This move is world famous. It was originally created by the player Deigo Maradona, hence the name. It is accomplished when the player with the ball approaches a defender puts their front foot on the ball and pulls it to their back foot while spinning around the defender.If you practice this you could potentially beat defenders easily like the all time famous Diego Maradona. Cruyff Turn:

This move is a really simple technique and yet it looks super cool on the field.It was originally performed by the player Johan Cruyff. It is done when the offender is dribbling with the ball with a defender flanking them. The offender takes the inside foot and plants it while taking the ball and dragging it back behind the standing leg, turning, and sprinting away. Click below to view how this is done.

Step Over:

This move is simple yet effective. It has been perfected by many world class players such as Cristiano Ronaldo, Messi, and Robinho.You don't even need to touch the ball! You simply move your foot around the ball while leaning one way to give the impression that you are going one way but in reality you are going the other. Then at the last second you tap the ball in the opposite direction you are leaning and accelerate away. Click below to see a video on how this is done.
