The Maradona

This move is world famous. It was originally created by the player Deigo Maradona, hence the name. It is accomplished when the player with the ball approaches a defender puts their front foot on the ball and pulls it to their back foot while spinning around the defender.If you practice this you could potentially beat defenders easily like the all time famous Diego Maradona.
